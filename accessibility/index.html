Accessibility Statement

T-Muzziic Group is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 AA where feasible.

Measures we take

  • Clear color contrast and legible type.
  • Semantic HTML with landmarks for screen readers.
  • Keyboard navigable controls.
  • Captions or descriptions on video content when provided by the platform.
